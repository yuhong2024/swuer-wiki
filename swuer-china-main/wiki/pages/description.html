YY peptide is an effective antifungal peptide that can specifically kill pathogenic mycelium forms of Candida albicans and also plays a vital role in maintaining the health of the gut microbiota, which prevents Candida albicans from becoming dangerous disease-causing forms. We use deep time convolutional networks to accelerate the discovery of antifungal peptides[1]. Then we learn to accurately identify antifungal peptides using AFP-MLF[2]. In addition, new antifungal peptides were identified using pre-trained seq2vec and 1DCNN-BiLSTM[1]. Finally, we predict minimum inhibitory concentration of antimicrobial peptides on ESKAPEE pathogens based on AI[3].

Some advice

We encourage you to put up a lot of information and content on your wiki, but we also encourage you to include summaries as much as possible. References

iGEM teams are encouraged to record references you use during the course of your research. They should be posted somewhere on your wiki so that judges and other visitors can see how you thought about your project and what works inspired you.
